A Few Ways to Get Started

Every engagement looks a little different, but most fall into one of these.

Quick Consult

One focused conversation to work through a specific problem. Good for a second opinion, a fast fix, or help thinking something through before you commit to a bigger project.

Project Engagement

A defined piece of work with a clear start and finish, like a new website, a pricing overhaul, or getting your systems to actually talk to each other. You'll know the plan and the cost before anything begins.

Ongoing Partnership

Continued support as your business grows. I stay involved on a regular basis, whether that's monthly check ins, ongoing data work, or just being the person you call when something comes up.

Whichever one fits, pricing is built around your business, and payment plans are always available.
